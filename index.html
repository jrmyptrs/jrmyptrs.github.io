Two Suns

WordPress Design & Development

Two Suns

twosuns.io

Two Suns is a passion project of mine, providing a hybrid experience of Star Wars news and affiliate shopping links. In addition to the design and development based off an existing WordPress theme, I write all content for the site.

The layout features news cards throughout the site, powered by a jQuery plugin called Masonry, which automatically aligns and arranges content into a grid.

Mobile

The news card layout adapts nicely to the mobile experience, with a sticky header allowing the user to quickly navigate around the site.

404 Page

The 404 Page is often times an afterthought on websites, so I wanted to bring some excitement to the page. I added some movement with an animation of an Imperial Probe Droid flying into the page to bring it to life.

Brooklyn Friends School

Front End Development & Project Management

Brooklyn Friends School

brooklynfriends.org

Brooklyn Friends School hired SMAKK Studios to complete re-design their existing website. As part of the SMAKK team, I worked first as a front end developer on the project.

With this being a digital platform for parents, students, and faculty to access, this was a massive project that took months of research, planning, and testing. The design team came up with many re-usable modules, so the development team decided to build out a Style Guide to catalog all of the common HTML and CSS.

Style Guide

Once the Style Guide was built, it was much a matter of building out the various page templates and layouts. As the project progressed, my role on the team shifted from developer to project manager. I oversaw the completion of the development including Q/A and testing.
