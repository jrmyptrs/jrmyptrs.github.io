Work Samples

Two Suns

WordPress Development & UI Design

Two Suns

Two Suns is a passion project of mine, providing a hybrid experience of Star Wars news and affiliate shopping links. In addition to the design and development based off an existing WordPress theme, I write all content for the site.

The card layout for the site is powered by a jQuery plugin called Masonry, which automatically aligns your content into a grid.

Mobile

The card layout adapts nicely to the mobile experience, with sticky navigation allowing the user to quickly navigate around the site.

404 Page

The 404 Page is often times an afterthought, so I wanted to bring some excitement to the page. I added some movement with an animation of an Imperial Probe Droid flying into the page.

Big Blue Interactive

Website Mock-Up & Visual Identity Refresh

Big Blue Interactive

UI Mock-Ups

Big Blue Interactive is a popular New York Giants fan site with a very active online community. With their current site looking quite outdated, I was eager to provide them with an alternative. This home page mock-up was built to spec based on a WordPress theme.

Visual Identity Refresh

In making the website layout more modern, I needed to do something with their existing visual identity. Because the logo could not change, I proceeded to update it to the current flat design trend.

NYC Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner Services, P.C.

NYC Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner Services, P.C.

NYC Psychiatric Nurse Practioner Services (NYCPNPS) provides mental health care in a modern, cozy environment. With a basement office located in Bushwick, NYCPNPS wanted a very modern, responsive website that provided all of the information a patient would need.

Paton for Congress

Website Mock-Up

Paton for Congress

In 2012, BRINKmedia was hired to design and develop a website for Jonathan Paton. Running for the House of Representatives in Arizona's 8th District, the former intelligence officer in the U.S. Army Reserve needed a website that would help engage and inform voters.

I created a mock-up that was chosen by Paton's team, and was also part of the front-end development on the website, which was built on WordPress and featured lots of social media engagement, multimedia, and events.
